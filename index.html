About Howling Wolf Trader's Treats

My family has had its share of food allergies. We are allergic to MSG, can’t tolerate anything soy, sometimes can’t handle gluten. We prefer to stay away from corn syrup. These things are found in almost everything store bought unless you wanna spend an arm and leg.


Avoiding these ingredients, we have created the sweets we desire out of healthy, simple, delicious ingredients.



Looking to custom make, whatever your heart desires. From flavored chocolates to gummy candies. Cookies, cakes, muffins, brownies, pies. We have over 40 different silicone gummy molds, a handful of plastic chocolate molds. We’re more than willing to work with you and get you whatever you desire.
